About

There are many creatures that live on our planet, all serving some kind of purpose for our planet. Despite this, many people consider these creatures to only cause harm because these creatures find their way into the houses of many. This has caused them to be given the title of "pests."

This website serves as a place to learn more about these so called "pests" and understand how they actually benefit our planet as decomposers, pollinators, and the many other roles they fill! I hope you enjoy your stay here and become an ally of these creatures that deserve some respect from human kind!

Featured Pests

Out of all of the pests that are discussed on my webpage, there are three that I feel stand above the rest. The first is the common spider, as its impact has been so beneficial for our lives and the health of the planet. The second mention goes to the cicada. Although scary and can be a nuisance with their noises, they are very helpful with plantlife. Finally we have the common fly, which are very helpful decomposers.
